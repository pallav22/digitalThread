Engine Failure Forecasting - Weibull Analysis

▶ Objective

● Forecasting based on Historical based modelling & diagnostic modelling

● Establishing relationship between diagnostic data & historical events pertaining to alerts, corresponding CNRs & SVs

● Leverage diagnostic data for improved forecasting along with the FM model for CMR

▶ Modelling Assumptions

● GE90 platform specific (Customers- Emirates, Cathay Pacific & Qatar )

● 8 years Data Set(2008 onwards)

● SEED data (referred for historical data , filtered for SVs having Full and QT , only)

● Numbers of Alerts and CNRs (referred for primary variables from diagnostic side)

● Mapping of Alerts-CNR-SEED data (for 394 unique ESNs)

● Modelling is for only "forthcoming SV/ ESN"

▶ Modelling Procedure :

● Alerts - Count of Alarms description, Flight Date and Time between consecutive SVs from the SEED data for each ESN. Categorization of the count of Alarms based on EGT, Fan Vibe, Oil/Fuel Issues on basis of alarms description

● CNRs - Count of Observations, Date Issued between Consecutive SVs from the SEED data for a particular ESN. Categorization of Count of observation description based on EGT, Chip Related Issues, Oil/Fuel Issues on basis of observation column description

● SEED Data - Filtering out ESNs for Emirates, Cathay Pacific, Qatar for Full and QT SVs having a SV post Jan '08.

● Weibull analysis from CNR data-

o Root cause mapping between two Shop visits (ie, SV1 to SV2)

o Identify top drivers for shop visit. Fuel/Oil issue and Fan/Core vibration are identified as top drivers.

o Create Weibull's for top drivers.

● Analyze the CNR Weibull impact in the CMR-

o Extract SV forecast for subject engines from the CMR run.

o Make new ICAM run for subject engines by adding CNR Weibull's along with Fleet model Weibull's which are used for CMR run.

o Analyze failure forecast changes because of CNR Weibull's

o Calculate accuracy of forecast when we include CNR Weibull's over CMR against actual shop visits.

▶ Results & Analysis :

● The CNR Weibulls predict's early shop visit over CMR forecast by 4 to 14 months.

● When we compare failure forecast VS actual shop visits happened in Jan to Jun'16, "The CMR+CNR" Weibull's predicts 62% accurate than CMR only Weibull's.
